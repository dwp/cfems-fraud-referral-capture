Report benefit fraud in Scotland

How you report someone in Scotland depends on the type of benefit they're getting.

We issue the following benefits to people living in Scotland:

  • Universal Credit
  • Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA)
  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income Support
  • State Pension
  • Pension Credit
  • Bereavement Support Payment

You can continue to report fraud of these benefits on GOV.UK.

For all other benefits you report fraud on myscot.gov.
