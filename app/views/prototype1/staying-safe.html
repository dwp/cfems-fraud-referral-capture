Staying safe

You do not have to give your name or contact details.

We do need the full name of the person you’re reporting.

The more information you can tell us the better.

For your safety you should not:

  • try to find out more than you already know
  • tell anyone you’re making a report
  • report something that would identify you to the person you're reporting

Investigations are confidential including the information you give us.

We cannot update you or tell you outcomes.
