Benefit fraud is deliberately claiming benefits you are not entitled, for example by:

  • giving false information
  • not reporting a change of circumstances

You can report someone to us if you think they’re committing benefit fraud.

You only need to report them once unless you have new information.

This page is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Reporting other types of fraud or crime

There’s a different way to report:

  • Child Benefit fraud
  • Tax fraud or tax avoidance, including tax credit fraud
  • social housing fraud (to the relevant council)
  • a crime
  • immigration or border crime
  • War pension Scheme or Armed Forces Compensation Scheme fraud (Veterans UK helpline)

Other ways to report

Contact our helpline if you cannot use the online service. Monday to Friday except bank holidays.

Do not send reports any other way, for example by post or webchat.
